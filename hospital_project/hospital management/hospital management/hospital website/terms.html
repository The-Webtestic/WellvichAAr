Terms & Conditions

Confidentiality: Your information is strictly confidential except in cases of harm to self or others.

Appointments: Sessions must be booked at least 2 days in advance. Clients are required to notify us at least 24 hours in advance for cancellations or rescheduling. While refunds will not be provided for cancellations, clients can reschedule their appointments if needed.

Payment: We accept payment only via credit/debit cards or electronic funds transfer at the time of the session.

Therapeutic Relationship: We work together with respect and trust towards agreed-upon goals.

Boundaries: We maintain professional boundaries and avoid dual relationships.

Emergencies: Seek immediate assistance in emergencies; we can provide support and resources.

Termination: Either party can end therapy at any time; referrals will be provided if needed.

Records: Records are securely maintained; clients can access them upon request.

Privacy Policy

Information Collection: We collect personal information for therapy purposes only.

Data Security: We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Confidentiality: Your information is kept confidential, except as required by law.

Data Retention: We retain your information as long as necessary and securely dispose of it afterward.

Third-party Disclosure: We do not share your information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your information.
